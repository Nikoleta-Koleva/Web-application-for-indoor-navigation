About

This indoor navigation application was done as a part of my Final Year Project. Its aim is to allow students to navigate easily towards reaching their tutor’s room inside Buckingham building. Currently, the development of the prototype will avoid requiring personal data and will work with dummy user data. For future work, the application will ask users to sign up to Google in order to access their timetable. The ethical, social and professional issues arising from the access of user data will be managed by Google.
